About us

Honesty and Affordability are what Pica's Automotive is based on for the last 24 years. Our AAA shop may look small, but it's not. Its 3000 square feet inside allows for every type of auto repair needed: foreign and domestic, which includes, but not limited to, oil changes, brakes, shocks, exhaust, check engine lights to name a few; steering, suspension, and alignments are our specialty. Here at Pica's Automotive we fix car's that the other shops can't fix. We actually do a lot of repairs for the local shops in the area. We also maintain your vehicles, keep records on every repair, and offer a 1 year warranty on all new parts. Once you are our customer, our goal is to take care of you and keep you happy. Pica's Automotive is not a corporate store where you are just a number. We are a small independent shop who appreciates your business. So whether you have a small or large problem, we are the #1 stop shop you need to be.
